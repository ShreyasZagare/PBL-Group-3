Where

The entertainment industry has always been volatile and changing. Gaming is not a brand-new industry, it has been around for quite some time in the form of physical games like chess, playing cards and even field sports. But it took a major leap as an entertainment product after it delved into the world of computers, turning into video gaming. Today, the video gaming industry is a bigger industry than the movie industry and the sports industry combined. It is valued at 180 billion $.
