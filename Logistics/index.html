Political Factors that Impact Logistics Industry.

Political factors play a significant role in determining the factors that can impact Logistics Industry.'s long term profitability in a certain country or market. Logistics Industry. is operating in Air Delivery & Freight Services in more than dozen countries and expose itself to different types of political environment and political system risks. The achieve success in such a dynamic Air Delivery & Freight Services industry across various countries is to diversify the systematic risks of political environment.

Logistics Industry. can closely analyze the following factors before entering or investing in a certain market-

1. Political stability and importance of Air Delivery & Freight Services sector in the country's economy.

2. Risk of military invasion

3. Level of corruption - especially levels of regulation in Services sector.

4. Bureaucracy and interference in Air Delivery & Freight Services industry by government.

5. Legal framework for contract enforcement

6. Intellectual property protection

7. Trade regulations & tariffs related to Services

8. Favored trading partners

9. Anti-trust laws related to Air Delivery & Freight Services

10. Pricing regulations – Are there any pricing regulatory mechanism for Services

11. Taxation - tax rates and incentives

12. Wage legislation - minimum wage and overtime

13. Work week regulations in Air Delivery & Freight Services

14. Mandatory employee benefits

15. Industrial safety regulations in the Services sector.

16. Product labeling and other requirements in Air Delivery & Freight Services

Technological Factors that Impact Logistics Industry.

Technology is fast disrupting various industries across the board. Transportation industry is a good case to illustrate this point. Over the last 5 years the industry has been transforming really fast, not even giving chance to the established players to cope with the changes. Taxi industry is now dominated by players like Uber and Lyft. Car industry is fast moving toward automation led by technology firm such as Google & manufacturing is disrupted by Tesla, which has stated an electronic car revolution. A firm should not only do technological analysis of the industry but also the speed at which technology disrupts that industry. Slow speed will give more time while fast speed of technological disruption may give a firm little time to cope and be profitable.



    Technology analysis involves understanding the following impacts -

    1. Recent technological developments by Logistics Industry. competitors

    2. Technology's impact on product offering

    3. Impact on cost structure in Air Delivery & Freight Services industry

    4. Impact on value chain structure in Services sector

    5. Rate of technological diffusion
